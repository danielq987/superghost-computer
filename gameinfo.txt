Superghost with Zyka!

For general rules of the game, visit https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Ghost_(game).

To play, start by inputting your name.

On each turn, you may either play a letter or challenge.

Play a letter with the following format: <letter> <before/after>
E.g: a before 
will insert an "a" before the current letters.

To challenge, enter: /ch
This will bring you to a series of prompts to verify the challenge.

Zyka will produce the optimal move at each turn. :D

Enjoy!
